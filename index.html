Memorandum of Understanding — Trustee Succession & Interest Redistribution

321 Phillips Way, Ellsworth, ME 04605  |  Est. 2005  |  Branch Lake
Why does order matter? The Flanagan Family 321 Trust is irrevocable. Article II only permits administrative modifications that do not materially affect beneficial interests. To stay within those boundaries, the transition must happen in a specific sequence — administrative steps first (Phases 1–3), then substantive changes through a proper legal mechanism (Phase 4), and finally a comprehensive record tying it all together (Phase 5). Skipping steps or reordering them could expose the process to legal challenge. Click each phase below to review the documents.
